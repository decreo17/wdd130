Rent Cards

Why use Central.cards
Renting for the cards or power that you need takes time and at times irritating, central.cards is here for you so resolve that problem, whether you are using bot to play for you, scholar manager who manage the rentals, multi-account player or just a casual player. Central.cards is free to use for you. Central.cards already earn fees when you rent using our platform, so no additional subscription fee is required.

Core Features

  • Buy and Rent Cards by hand
  • Automatically buy/rent a specific amount of collection power
  • Automatically buy/rent specific cards/lineups
  • Manage(View/Add/Remove, maybe edit) automatic card renting
  • Manage(View/Add/Remove, maybe edit) automatic card buying
  • Option to rent/buy for power or to rent specific cards OR whole decks
  • A lot of helpful filters for cards
  • No new sign up needed, login using your Splinterlands Hive account safely using HiveKeychain
  • We will not need or store any private keys, just login using HiveKeychain safely.
  • All automatic transactions will be possible through granted rental/buy authorities, consent is needed for you to activate this and you could stop this anytime!

In the future

  • Purchase/Rental History
  • Manage your own cards (low priority)
  • Sell/Rent out your own cards (low priority)
  • Features that will be frequently requested by users
